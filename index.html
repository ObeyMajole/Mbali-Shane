First Holiday

11 Dec 2020

Our first holiday in Northern Cape. Shane made the decision to drive in order to rescue his partner, who had lost her wallet with all of her cards. Mbalenhle had informed Shane that she was going to have to walk to work the following day as she didn't have enough cash for a cab fare after losing her wallet. Shane made the decision at 1am to leave Johannesburg and travel to the Northern Cape, where he drove over potholed roads in an attempt to surprise her before she could leave for work at 7:00am. They ultimately made the decision to go to the desert, so the weekend turned into a vacation.

Proposal

23rd Sep 2023

The groom’s uncles sent a letter “ba kopa sego sa metsi” to the bride’s family, on the 31st of July. On the 23rd of September 2023, the groom’s uncles went to the bride’s family to negotiate and ask for hand in marriage between Shane and Mbalenhle. The groom’s family sent another letter end of Mid-March 2024 to bride’s family, requesting to come and conclude the lobola on the 6th of April 2024. On the 6th of April, the families met again and concluded on the lobola, and the wedding date was then set
